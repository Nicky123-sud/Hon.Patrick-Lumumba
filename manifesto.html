🗳️ Our Manifesto

A Comprehensive Vision for Kitutu Chache South Development

"Building a Future That Cares – For All"

🎯 Vision Statement

To transform Kitutu Chache South into a model constituency characterized by prosperity, opportunity, and sustainable development.

💡
Innovation & Progress

Embracing modern solutions for community development

🤝
Inclusive Development

Ensuring no one is left behind in our progress

🌱
Sustainable Growth

Building for future generations

❤️
Community Welfare

Putting people first in all decisions

📋 Key Agenda Items

🏫

Education for All

Expand access to quality, affordable education — including bursaries and new schools in underserved wards.

  • School infrastructure upgrades
  • Digital learning facilities
  • Teacher development programs
  • Scholarship programs
👨‍🎓

Youth Empowerment

Create job opportunities, promote ICT hubs, fund sports talent, and support entrepreneurship for youth.

  • Skills training centers
  • ICT innovation hubs
  • Sports development
  • Entrepreneurship support
🛣️

Infrastructure

Tarmac rural roads, improve drainage, and ensure every ward has street lighting and safe bridges.

  • Road tarmacking
  • Drainage systems
  • Street lighting
  • Bridge construction
🏥

Healthcare Access

Upgrade health centres, equip dispensaries, and ensure free maternal and child healthcare services.

  • Health facility upgrades
  • Medical equipment
  • Maternal care
  • Child healthcare
🌾

Agriculture Support

Offer subsidies, modern tools, and extension services to support local farmers and boost food security.

  • Farming subsidies
  • Modern tools
  • Extension services
  • Market access
💻

Digital Governance

Digitize public service access, promote transparency, and connect citizens through e-governance platforms.

  • E-governance platforms
  • Digital services
  • Transparency tools
  • Citizen engagement
