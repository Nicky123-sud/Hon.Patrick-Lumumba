Kitutu Chache South Constituency

üìå Context & Why This Matters

Population Pressure

Kitutu Chache South is one of the most densely populated constituencies in Kisii, with over 160,000 residents on a small land area. This creates pressure on land, infrastructure, and water resources.

Poverty Levels

Around 45% of households in Kisii County live below the poverty line, with rural poverty and unemployment rates higher among women and youth.

Youth & Women Demographics

Youth (18‚Äì35) and women form the largest population group but remain underrepresented in economic opportunities.

Agriculture & Land Fragmentation

Most families rely on small-scale farming, yet productivity is low due to outdated methods, poor infrastructure, and lack of value addition.

Water & Environment

Rivers and streams are heavily polluted due to encroachment and poor waste management, threatening health and agriculture.

Transport & Connectivity

Poor road conditions and lack of rural electrification hinder businesses, especially boda boda riders and cottage industries.
