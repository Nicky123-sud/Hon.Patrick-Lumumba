Hon. Patrick Lumumba is a seasoned public administrator and community leader with over 15 years of experience in government service and community development.

His vision is to transform Kitutu Chache South into a model constituency characterized by prosperity, opportunity, and sustainable development. Through his extensive experience in public administration, he understands the needs of the people and has the expertise to deliver meaningful change.
